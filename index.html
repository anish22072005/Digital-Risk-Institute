About IDR

Training the Next Generation of Risk Professionals

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber and technology risk roles across regulated, high-consequence environments. We bridge the gap between academic insight and operational readiness.

Through a unique model combining UK university partnership with live industry projects, IDR produces practitioners who are immediately effective in financial services, critical infrastructure, and emerging technology sectors. Our programmes are designed with employers — not just for them.

What We Do

Our Core Service Model

Three interconnected pillars that move talent from learning to deployment.

01 — Academy

Academy

Structured training programmes and intensive bootcamps for students and professionals seeking skills in digital and cyber risk, AI governance, and regulatory frameworks.

02 — Innovation

Innovation & Incubation

Developing intellectual property, future risk models, and AI governance frameworks — incubating ideas that reshape how organisations understand and manage emerging threats.

Advisory Services
